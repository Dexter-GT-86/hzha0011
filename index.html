what is Bungy

The jumper stands on the main axis with a height of more than 40 meters (equivalent to 10 stories), on the top of a tower, a tall building, a crane, or even a hot air balloon. A long rubber strip fixed at one end is tied to the joint and both arms stretched out, With legs close together, jumped head down.

The rubber strip tied to the jumper's ankle is long enough to allow the jumper to enjoy a few seconds of "free fall" in the air. When the human body falls to a certain distance from the ground, the rubber rope is pulled and tightened to prevent the human body from falling. When it reaches the lowest point, the rubber bounces again, the person is pulled up, and then falls again. Repeat this many times until the rubber Until the elasticity of the rope disappears, this is the whole process of bungee jumping.
